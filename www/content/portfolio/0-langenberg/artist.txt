Title: Langenberg
----
Text: Langenberg is co-founder of Essen based label Mild Pitch. His first output as a producer in 2007 marked the beginning of an ongoing string of remarkable releases on labels like Mild Pitch, Drumpoet Community, STHLM Audio, Liebe*Detail & Dessous Recordings. Under his „Ribn“ moniker, a producing alliance with Manuel Tur, he shows an equally attraktive discographie with releases on labels like Mule Electronic, Styrax Leaves, Millions of Moments (MOM), Mild Pitch, Ovum & Poker Flat Recordings.

He lives and works in Berlin / Germany.
