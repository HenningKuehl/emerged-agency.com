Joshua Leary, better known by the stage name Evian Christ, released his debut mixtape Kings and Them in 2012 and gained prominence as a collaborator on Kanye West’s 2013 album Yeezus. He released a further EP on Tri Angle, Waterfall, in 2014 and will release another EP in early 2017 via Warp. His Debut LP will be out in Spring 2017 via Warp.
