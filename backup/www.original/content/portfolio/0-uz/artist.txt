UZ is one of the stage names of Guillaume Barbier who is also known as DJ TRoubl – the winner of France’s DJ championship in 2000 and he also won several awards in Europe. In 2002, he won the International Turntable Federation Battle held in Munich as well as the All-Star Beatdown Battle which happened in New York.

In 2014, he released the single “E-Motional Love.” The single “Particles of Light” dropped in 2016 while the three-track EP Moments of Life was released in 2017. In the same year, he released his debut album, Layers.
The said release includes the tracks “Moment,” “Burst,” and “Beautiful Sunrise.” He was also the featured artist on Tre Dilly’s single “I Got Chu.”  Some of the artists he collaborated with include DJ Shadow, Ice Cube, T-Pain, and Boys Noize.
