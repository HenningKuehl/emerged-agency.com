Knxwledge (pronounced “knowledge”) is a beat maker raised in central New Jersey and Philadelphia, living and working in Los Angeles. His recent work alongside Anderson.Paak as the duo NxWorries has received critical acclaim. In March 2020 Knxwledge released 1988, his second album for Stones Throw.
