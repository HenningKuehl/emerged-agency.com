Text: 

Ash Walker built a reputation as a fiercely underground DJ with his deep selections of soul, jazz, rhythm & blues, latin hitters and complimentary roots & culture selections, livening up parties worldwide with a heavyweight astral shower of rhythm and vibes.

Dissatisfied with the familiarity of many gigs today Ash has collaborated with Ezra Lloyd-Jackson to turn each performance of The Ash Walker Experience into a vibrant, multi-sensory experience combining projections and carefully crafted frequency complimenting fragrances to create a rich, unique and immersive live experience.
