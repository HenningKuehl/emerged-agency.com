Text: 

Michael Silver, better known as CFCF, is a celebrated electronic music producer from Montreal, Quebec. 
His eclectic discography spans house, new age collage, balearic pop, modern classical, synth-pop, leftfield disco, and everything in between. 
In recent years, CFCF has increasingly focused on producing floor-centric house and techno, as can be heard on his latest releases. 

He is equally acclaimed for his work as a film composer and remixer, and earned a 2016 Grammy Award nomination for his remix of Max Richter’s “Berlin By Overnight”.
